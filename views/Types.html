The Diverse Forms of Iron Warriors

Robots are divided into two categories: Industrial Robot and Special-purpose Robot.

Industrial Robot

Multi-jointed manipulators or multi-degree-of-freedom robots for industrial applications

Special-purpose Robot

Robots applied in professional fields, which are generally operated or used by personnel who have received specialized training, to assist and/or replace humans in performing tasks
